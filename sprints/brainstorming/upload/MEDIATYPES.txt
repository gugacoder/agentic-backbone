FORMATOS DE ARQUIVO SUPORTADOS PARA UPLOAD
===========================================

----------------------------------------------------------------

image/jpeg
image/gif
image/webp
application/pdf
audio/wav
audio/mp3
audio/mpeg
text/plain
text/csv
application/json

PRE-PROCESSADO (convertido para texto antes de enviar)
------------------------------------------------------

application/vnd.openxmlformats-officedocument.spreadsheetml.sheet         (.xlsx)
